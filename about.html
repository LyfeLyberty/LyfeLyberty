About Us

The Lyfe & Lyberty Foundation has been started with a purpose of creating a spark among the people to claim their Life and Liberty. The aim is to deliberate upon and develop solutions for the social and legal challenges that are being faced by the Indian citizen. India had claimed independence from foreign rule on August 15, 1947 but true freedom has yet not been achieved. It can only be achieved when every Indian citizen is free from oppression and exploitation and has the liberty to live a life with human dignity.

The Indian Constitution introduced Articles 19 & 21, which ensure the protection of the right to life and liberty of all Indian citizens. This can only be accomplished when every Indian citizen stands in solidarity with their fellow citizen to fight for their liberty to live a life of human dignity. That's the goal, to achieve independence from oppression and exploitation and to lead the world towards creating a better future by establishing an example of Life and Liberty in India.

The introduction of the letter ‘y’ instead of ‘i’ has been deliberately done to signify that this foundation do not engage in mere intellectual bickering but rather focuses on two major objectives:

  • Discuss
  • Develop

We wish to create opportunities and processes which encourage people to question the present status quo and ask the question “Why” whenever their right to life and liberty is encroached upon. And, also not to stop at this stage but to go ahead and find solutions to the problems, which hinder their access to their right of life and liberty.
